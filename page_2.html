MY HERO ACADEMIA (僕のヒーローアカデミア)


My Hero Academia is the story of a boy named Izuku Midoriya (nickname deku, the word deku referred to a certain doll with no arms or legs and used as an insult meaning someone is useless). In the TV show we can see Deku being a kind and loving boy with such a bright POV of the world, filled with rainbows and cupcakes. Altho he centered his life around one thing in life being a superhero. In the world deku lives in anyone with what is called a ‘quirk’ can be a hero. A quirk is a superhero that people are inborn with 80% of the population was born with there weren't a lot of options for people who had a weak quirk or were quirkless, but despite being quirkless Deku looked up to all sorts of heroes all with a different kind of different powers.The one he specifically looks up to is a hero named All Might the #1 ranked hero in the world. Deku wants to hopefully be #2 ranked hero with his best friend ‘kacchan’ being #1 hero. Kacchan (or his real name Katsuki) isn't really the nicest person in the My Hero Academia world and isn't the best to deku (kacchan the one who gave izuku the nickname) when deku told kacchan that he was quirkless in just grade school, at first kacchan told him that deku would be the first quirkless hero. That’s why deku stayed friends with kacchan, because kacchan at the time believed that deku could be a hero, but when middle school hit kacchan knew deku could never be a hero. Therefore saw deku as his nickname stands by a good for nothing object in the way of his future of his hero career...
