ASTRO BOY (アトム)



Astro is a robot boy. He was made by Ochanomizu at the Ministry of science. He is different from normal robots in that he has Kokoro(Kokoro means he can feel, develop his own thoughts, feelings and has a mind of his own). Astro learns at an exponential rate, which means he soaks knowledge up like a sponge, this also means he needs to be taught lessons very often by his nanny named Nora. As Astro learns more and more about his robot body the more features and powers he figures out and discovers. The whole metro city (where astro lives) knows the wonders of the robot boy. But something is always off about Ochanomizu; he is always surprised when Astro gets a new ability and when the public asks Dr. Ochanomizu about how astro works, how he gets all his powers and, if there are any more powers that he can unlock...
